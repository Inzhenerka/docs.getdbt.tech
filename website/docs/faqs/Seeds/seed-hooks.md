- [pre-hooks & post-hooks](reference/resource-configs/pre-hook-post-hook.md)
- [on-run-start & on-run-end hooks](reference/project-configs/on-run-start-on-run-end.md)

Настройте их в вашем файле `dbt_project.yml`.
